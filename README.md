# -Impact-of-Influencer-Marketing-on-Purchase-Behaviour-of-Gen-Z-Consumers-in-India

Project Overview

This research project investigates how influencer marketing affects the purchasing decisions of Generation Z consumers in India. With the rapid growth of social media platforms such as Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and Snapchat, influencers have become powerful opinion leaders capable of shaping consumer attitudes, preferences, and buying behavior.

The study explores the relationship between influencer characteristics, consumer trust, engagement levels, and purchase intentions among Gen Z consumers, who represent one of the most digitally connected and socially active generations.

Research Background

Traditional advertising methods are gradually losing effectiveness among younger consumers. Generation Z tends to rely more on recommendations, reviews, and experiences shared by influencers rather than conventional advertisements.

Influencer marketing has emerged as a modern marketing strategy where brands collaborate with social media influencers to promote products and services. These influencers build strong relationships with their followers through authentic content, making their recommendations highly persuasive.

Research Problem

Despite the increasing popularity of influencer marketing, there is a need to understand:

How influencer marketing affects Gen Z purchasing decisions.
Which influencer characteristics most strongly influence buying behavior.
The role of trust and credibility in influencer-driven marketing.
How social media engagement impacts purchase intentions.

This study aims to bridge this gap by examining the purchasing behavior of Gen Z consumers in the Indian market.

Research Objectives

Factors Influencing Purchase Behaviour
1. Trust and Credibility

Consumers are more likely to purchase products recommended by influencers they trust.

Impact

Higher credibility leads to stronger purchase intentions.

2. Authenticity

Authentic and genuine recommendations increase consumer confidence.

Impact

Consumers perceive authentic influencers as more reliable sources of information.

3. Content Quality

High-quality content attracts attention and improves audience engagement.

Impact

Better content increases product awareness and purchase consideration.

4. Social Proof

Large follower counts and positive audience interactions influence consumer perceptions.

Impact

Consumers often view popular products as more trustworthy and desirable.

5. Engagement Rate

The level of interaction between influencers and followers affects purchasing decisions.

Impact

Higher engagement often results in stronger consumer influence.
